Free Analytics for Your Publii Blog – Know Your Readers Without Creeping Them Out

Even a simple blog can benefit from analytics. Knowing which posts are popular, where visitors come from, and how they navigate your site helps you create content that resonates. With static websites, traditional analytics tools often feel like overkill, but modern privacy-focused solutions work beautifully.

Privacy-Friendly Analytics

Tools like Cloudflare Web Analytics, Umami, and GoatCounter provide insights without tracking users with cookies. This means no pop-ups or GDPR banners, and your readers enjoy a clean, uninterrupted experience. Despite being lightweight, these tools still provide meaningful data on page views, popular posts, and traffic sources.

How It Works With Publii

Adding analytics to Publii is surprisingly easy. You simply paste a short script into the custom HTML header section. Publii ensures it’s included on every page when you sync. No database setup, no plugins, no technical headaches. Your blog stays static, fast, and secure, while still giving you valuable insights.

Why It’s Motivating

Seeing your content reach people is inspiring. Even knowing that a handful of readers enjoy your posts can motivate you to write more. Analytics shouldn’t feel like spying; it should feel like feedback — a gentle way to understand your audience and refine your content.

Free Tools That Work Well

  • Cloudflare Web Analytics: Free, fast, and cookie-free.

  • Umami: Open-source and clean, with optional self-hosting.

  • GoatCounter: Lightweight, free for personal use, and simple to implement.

These tools allow you to track engagement, understand patterns, and make your blog more enjoyable — all while keeping your readers’ privacy intact.
