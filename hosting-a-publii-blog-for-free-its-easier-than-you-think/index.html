Hosting a Publii Blog for Free – It’s Easier Than You Think

One of the most exciting things about Publii is how easy it makes hosting a website. Because it’s static, you don’t need expensive servers or complex backend setups. You can get your blog online for free, in minutes, and still have a professional, fast, and secure site.

Hosting Options for Free

There are several free hosting services that work perfectly with Publii. GitHub Pages, Netlify, and Vercel are among the most popular. Each offers its own advantages, but the core idea is the same: upload your pre-built static files, and your website is live.

GitHub Pages

GitHub Pages is perfect if you’re familiar with version control. You create a repository, push your static files, and GitHub serves your website for free. You can even use a custom domain. The process is simple once you understand the basics of repositories and commits.

Netlify

Netlify adds an extra layer of simplicity. It connects directly with Publii, supports automatic SSL, and deploys your site globally via a CDN. You can even set up automatic updates whenever you change your local files. It’s perfect for those who want a hands-off experience after the initial setup.

Vercel

Vercel is another excellent option, especially if you plan to explore more advanced static sites in the future. It provides fast global hosting and integrates easily with Publii’s sync feature. Custom domains and HTTPS are included, giving your site a professional polish.

Why Free Hosting Feels Different

Using free hosting for a Publii blog feels empowering. You don’t worry about server crashes, database errors, or monthly fees. Instead, you can focus entirely on creating content. Every time you hit “Sync,” your website magically appears online, secure and fast. For bloggers just starting out, this freedom is priceless.
