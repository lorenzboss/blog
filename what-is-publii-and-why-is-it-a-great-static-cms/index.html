Why I Love Publii (and You Might Too)

If you’ve ever been frustrated by slow CMS dashboards, endless updates, or constant security patches, Publii might feel like a breath of fresh air. Unlike traditional CMSs, Publii works locally on your desktop. You write your posts, select a theme, tweak some settings, and when you’re ready, it generates a full static website that can be uploaded anywhere. Fast, secure, and surprisingly elegant.

How Publii Works

Publii operates offline. You don’t need a web server or database to start. Everything happens locally on your computer. When you click Sync, your entire site — posts, pages, images, and settings — is converted into static files. You can then upload your site using FTP, SFTP, GitHub Pages, Netlify, or any other compatible service. This approach not only improves speed but also drastically reduces security risks.

Why It Feels Different

What makes Publii special is the balance between simplicity and control. Unlike some static site generators that require you to write Markdown or code templates, Publii provides a polished, user-friendly interface. You can focus on writing, designing, and sharing your content, while Publii handles the technical side. Offline editing also means you can work anywhere — on a train, in a café, or during a hike — without worrying about internet access.

Who Develops It

Publii is developed by TidyCustoms, an independent team from Poland. Their focus is on simplicity, security, and user experience. The project is actively maintained, and the community around it is supportive and enthusiastic. The team provides a growing collection of themes and helpful documentation, making it approachable even for beginners.

Why You Might Choose Publii

Publii is ideal for bloggers, creatives, and small business owners who want a website that is:

  • Fast: Pre-generated pages load almost instantly.

  • Secure: No database, no login page — minimal risk of hacking.

  • Low-maintenance: No server updates or complicated plugins required.

  • Flexible: Publish anywhere, customize themes, and work offline.

For me, Publii has transformed how I think about blogging. It turns what could be a stressful technical task into a smooth creative workflow. For anyone tired of fighting with complex CMSs, it’s a joy to use.
